A–Z index

ACILMOPRST

A

Auto-UpdateRADCounter checks for new versions on startup and updates itself in place — no installer.

C

CDP SetupOne-click provisioning that sets up the Clario and Mosaic CDP connections RADCounter uses to read work units and the current study.Clario CDP ReaderThe connection RADCounter uses to read Clario directly — the per-study work unit, patient class, and priority.Clario Compare ViewA statistics view that pits your totals against Clario's over studies that have both — with a compensation delta, drafted split, and a list of study types that differ.Clario Work-Unit ComparisonEach study shows RADCounter's TBWU in blue with Clario's own per-study work unit in white directly underneath.Cloud BackupOptionally back up your records to OneDrive or Dropbox so a shift's history survives a machine change.Critical ResultsStudies with a communicated critical result are flagged on the record, carried over from MosaicTools.

I

Integration Status LightsThe bottom status bar is three at-a-glance lights — Clario CDP, Mosaic CDP, MosaicTools — green when live, red when down.

L

Live RVU TrackingYour RVU total and per-hour rate, updating on the main bar the moment each study completes.Live TBWU TrackingThe time-based work-unit total and rate — RADCounter's headline productivity metric — live on the main bar.Local Records DatabaseEvery study and shift is stored in a local SQLite database on your machine — the source for all statistics.

M

Mosaic CDP ReaderReads the current study straight from Mosaic when the MosaicTools bridge is down — a more reliable fallback than the accessibility tree.MosaicTools BridgeRADCounter's primary link to what you're reading — a named-pipe connection to MosaicTools that reports the current study and sign/discard events.

O

Official Signed TimeRADCounter records Clario's official signed date and time for each study, used to compare compensation against the exact rate hour.

P

Pace CarA live "+2.1 ahead / -1.4 behind" readout telling you at a glance whether you're beating your pace.Patient-Class AccuracyHolds the last-known-good patient class for a study and back-fills it if it arrives late, so completed studies don't land as "Unknown."Payroll ReconcileStraighten out shift boundaries after the fact so your history and totals line up with what you actually worked.Private Accession HandlingAccessions are stored hashed, never raw, and patient information is kept in memory only — never written to disk.Projected IncomeLive and projected earnings for the shift, priced from your TBWU at the current rate schedule.

R

Recent StudiesA running list of the studies you've just signed, each with its TBWU, how long ago it was, and read duration.RVU / StudyAn optional readout of your average RVU per study — a quick read on case intensity.

S

Shifts & Auto-ResumeRADCounter groups your work into shifts and can resume the current one automatically when you relaunch.Statistics & ChartsA statistics window with about a dozen view modes and charts — trends by hour, day, modality, and more — over any date range you pick.Study MixBreaks a shift or date range down by modality and study type, so you can see the composition of what you read.

T

ThemesTwenty-plus color presets plus a custom theme, with dark and light options and an always-on-top mode.
