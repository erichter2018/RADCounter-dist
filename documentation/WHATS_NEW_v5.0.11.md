# What's New in RADCounter 5.0.11

## Draft Impact (Statistics)
A new **Draft Impact** tab in Statistics compares how long studies take to read when a draft report already existed versus when there was none.

- Broken down by **study type** and **patient class** (Stroke, Trauma, Emergency, Inpatient, Outpatient)
- An **outlier slider** trims the fastest and slowest studies from each group before averaging
- **Overall and per-study-type read-time averages** show immediately from your history
- A **Deltas only** toggle hides study counts and read times so the comparison is safe to share
- A **Copy deltas** button puts the comparison on your clipboard as text

Drafted status is now recorded for each study (captured live from MosaicTools). The drafted-vs-not comparisons build up going forward; older studies have no recorded draft status, so they show under the plain averages only.

## Statistics fixes
- Clicking a column header no longer scrambles the report sections
- Removed the empty space on the right side of table-only views
- More readable buttons; value columns now have right-aligned headers
