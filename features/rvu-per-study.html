RVU / Study

An optional readout of your average RVU per study — a quick read on case intensity.

What it does

Shows your average RVU per study for the shift — simply your total RVU divided by study count. It's a fast gauge of whether you're on a run of heavy cross-sectional work or lighter plain-film studies.

How to turn it on / set it up

Enable the readout under Settings → Display. It's off by default.

Using it

The average updates as studies complete. Watched next to your per-hour rate, it separates "reading fast" from "reading heavy."

Tips & gotchas

  • This is a productivity read, not a compensation figure — there's no dollar column attached to it.
  • For a fuller breakdown of what you've read by modality and type, see [[study-mix]].
