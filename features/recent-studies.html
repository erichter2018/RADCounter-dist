Recent Studies

A running list of the studies you've just signed, each with its TBWU, how long ago it was, and read duration.

What it does

Recent Studies is the rolling list of what you've just finished. Each row reads as two tidy lines: the procedure and your TBWU on top, and "7 minutes ago, 1m 19s read duration" with Clario's TBWU on the line below.

Using it

A study appears the instant it's signed — no waiting for the next scan. RADCounter's TBWU shows in blue; if Clario CDP is on, Clario's own number fills in white underneath once it finalizes (see [[clario-wu-compare]]).

Tips & gotchas

  • The read-duration figure is how long the study was open, useful for spotting the case that ate your morning.
  • A study you discard never appears here, and a study won't jump into the list until you've actually signed it — sign/discard signals are interpreted carefully to keep the list honest.
