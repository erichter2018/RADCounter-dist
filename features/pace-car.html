Pace Car

A live "+2.1 ahead / -1.4 behind" readout telling you at a glance whether you're beating your pace.

What it does

The pace car compares your current rate against a target and shows the gap — for example +2.1 ahead or -1.4 behind — so you always know where you stand without doing the math.

How to turn it on / set it up

The pace comparison is configured on the Compensation tab. Turn on Pace vs prior shift to benchmark against your last shift's rate instead of a fixed goal.

Using it

As the shift goes on the pace figure updates every time a study lands. Early in a shift it swings a lot on each study; it settles as your sample grows.

Tips & gotchas

  • Pace is most useful after the first hour, once your per-hour rate has stabilized.
  • Pair it with Est Total from [[live-tbwu]] to see both the rate gap and where you'll finish.
