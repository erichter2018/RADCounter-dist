Critical Results

Studies with a communicated critical result are flagged on the record, carried over from MosaicTools.

What it does

When a study carries a critical result, that flag is captured alongside the study so it's recorded with your work rather than lost. The signal comes from MosaicTools' critical-results workflow over the bridge.

Using it

The flag rides along automatically while the MosaicTools bridge is connected — no separate step. It's stored on the study's record for later reference.

Tips & gotchas

  • This depends on the [[mosaictools-bridge]]; without MosaicTools running, the critical flag isn't available.
  • No patient identifiers are stored with the flag — see [[accession-privacy]] for how RADCounter handles study identity.
