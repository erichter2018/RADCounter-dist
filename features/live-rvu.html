Live RVU Tracking

Your RVU total and per-hour rate, updating on the main bar the moment each study completes.

What it does

RADCounter counts the Relative Value Units of every study you read and keeps a running total on the main bar, alongside your per-hour rate for the current shift. Each figure moves the instant a study is detected as complete — no refresh, no manual entry.

Using it

The RVU readout sits on the compact main bar so it stays visible next to your worklist. RADCounter derives each study's RVU from its procedure using the bundled rvu_rules.yaml/tbwu_rules.db value tables, so a multi-accession or bilateral study is scored the way it's actually credited.

Tips & gotchas

  • RVU is the classic metric; TBWU is the newer per-hour-weighted unit RADCounter also tracks — see [[live-tbwu]]. You can show either or both.
  • The total is per shift. When a new shift is detected the counters reset and the finished shift is saved to your local history.
