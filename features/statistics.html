Statistics & Charts

A statistics window with about a dozen view modes and charts — trends by hour, day, modality, and more — over any date range you pick.

What it does

The Statistics window turns your saved shift history into charts and tables. It offers roughly a dozen view modes — breakdowns by hour of day, by day, by modality and study type, pace over time, and the Clario comparison — each over a date range you choose.

Using it

Open Statistics, pick a period, and switch view modes to slice the same data different ways. Charts render inline so you can spot trends across shifts, not just within one.

Tips & gotchas

  • All of it runs off your local records database — nothing leaves your machine.
  • Two views worth knowing: [[study-mix]] for what you read, and [[clario-compare-view]] for how your numbers line up against Clario's.
