Clario Compare View

A statistics view that pits your totals against Clario's over studies that have both — with a compensation delta, drafted split, and a list of study types that differ.

What it does

A view in the Statistics window that, for the period you pick, compares your numbers against Clario's over every study that has both values. It has three parts:

  • Compensation delta at the top — priced with your TBWU on both sides, so the difference isolates the timestamp effect: your detected completion time versus Clario's official signed time landing in different rate hours.
  • Work-unit totals with a drafted / not-drafted split.
  • Study types that differ — procedures where your TBWU and Clario's work unit diverge by more than a hundredth per study, so genuine discrepancies surface while rounding noise stays hidden.

How to turn it on / set it up

It's the last view in the Statistics window, and it populates once Clario CDP is enabled and studies have both numbers recorded. See [[cdp-setup]].

Tips & gotchas

  • Because both sides are priced with your TBWU, a non-zero compensation delta is purely a timing difference, not a valuation difference.
  • A study type that shows up under "differ" usually reflects a patient-class or location adjustment mismatch worth checking against your rate sheet.
