RADCounter

Real-time RVU & TBWU tracking for the radiology reading room

RADCounter is a lightweight desktop companion that counts your work as you read it — live RVU and TBWU totals, per-hour pace against a goal, projected end-of-shift income, and a full statistics view of your shifts. It identifies each study straight from Mosaic and Clario, compares its own work units against Clario's, and keeps a private local record of every shift. One small exe, no installer, auto-updating.

✨ New in v5.1.0

v5.1.0Clario Compare & CDP
  • Clario work-unit comparison: each study now shows RADCounter's TBWU with Clario's own per-study number underneath, so you can see where they agree and where they don't. Display-and-compare only — it never changes your…
  • New "Clario Compare" statistics view: a compensation delta priced with your TBWU on both sides (isolating the timestamp effect), work-unit totals, a drafted / not-drafted split, and a list of study types that diverge …
  • RADCounter can now read the current study straight from Mosaic when the MosaicTools bridge is down (opt-in Mosaic CDP reader).
  • The bottom status bar is now three at-a-glance liveness lights — Clario CDP, Mosaic CDP, and MosaicTools — green when live, red when down.
  • Recent Studies redesigned: procedure and your TBWU on top, then "X ago, Ym Zs read duration" with Clario's number below.
  • Fix: completed studies no longer land as patient class "Unknown" — the last-known-good class is held and back-filled if it arrives late.
  • Fix: discarded studies aren't counted, and a signed study no longer jumps into Recent Studies before you've actually signed it.
v5.0.21Instant Recent Studies & Study Mix
  • Recent Studies updates the instant a study is signed instead of waiting for the next scan.
  • Studies complete on the sign action itself for more accurate end-of-study timestamps.
  • New Study Mix view breaks your shift down by modality and study type.
v5.0.20Reconcile fix & current-rate income
  • Payroll reconcile no longer creates overlapping or multi-day shifts.
  • Month income is projected at your current rate schedule rather than trailing history.
