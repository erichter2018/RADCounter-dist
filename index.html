RADCounter

Real-time RVU & TBWU tracking for the radiology reading room

RADCounter is a lightweight desktop companion that counts your work as you read it — live RVU and TBWU totals, per-hour pace against a goal, projected end-of-shift income, and a full statistics view of your shifts. It identifies each study straight from Mosaic and Clario, compares its own work units against Clario's, and keeps a private local record of every shift. One small exe, no installer, auto-updating.
